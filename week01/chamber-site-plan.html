Overview

Site Name

Greater Federal Way Chamber of Commerce. This name best describes the area that the chamber serves.

Site Purpose

The Greater Federal Way Chamber of Commerce fosters economic interests within the city of Federal Way and the surrounding smaller communities.

The Chamber encourages business, economic and community development through member services and involvement in public policy.
